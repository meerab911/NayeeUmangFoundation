About Nayee Umang

Founded in 2010 by visionary philanthropist Noreen Aziel, Nayee Umang Organization believes that every individual deserves equal access to education, healthcare, and opportunities for growth.

Our foundation works closely with local communities and partners to create sustainable initiatives that empower people to lead healthier, more educated, and dignified lives.

We proudly stand with Pakistan’s Christian community—supporting schools, clinics, and neighborhood outreach so that every family, regardless of faith, can access hope and opportunity.

Mission

To bridge social gaps by delivering holistic support across education, health, and social welfare.

Vision

Transform communities through inclusive growth and lasting impact.

Our Journey to Impact

A timeline of milestones that shaped Nayee Umang’s growth and deepened our service to communities across Pakistan.

Partner With Us
2010

Foundation Established

Noreen Aziel launches Nayee Umang with a scholarship fund for girls in Karachi and Lahore’s Christian quarters.

2015

Community Health Network

Mobile clinics expand to Sindh and Punjab, offering maternal health and telemedicine to over 40 villages.

2019

Digital Skills Labs

Launch of digital literacy labs and livelihood incubators for youth and women entrepreneurs.

2024

National Solidarity Coalitions

Strategic alliances with Christian councils, youth groups, and corporate partners accelerate our goal of serving 25 new communities by 2025.
